About Us

Crafting Stunning Ceiling Designs Since 2005

With over 15 years of expertise in POP and wall designing, Durgesh Pop Centre has established itself as a leader in the industry. We specialize in crafting elegant, durable, and stunning designs for homes and businesses.

Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional techniques with modern innovations to create ceiling designs that stand the test of time. Every project we undertake is executed with precision, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of ceiling design solutions to meet your specific needs and preferences.

POP Half Ceiling
